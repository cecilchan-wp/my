Janice's Floral Art and Design is a online E-Learning platform which founded in 2018by Janice Chong. Designed to be an place for inspiration for people who love floral art. We are offered online course for Floral Design and aims to nurture teaching and floristry skills amongst participants. Participants will be trained to ultimately conduct professional and high quality floral design course.

Turn your passion of floriculture into an exciting and rewarding career. Let's it be the thing that fulfil your creative desire, sheer of self enjoyment or your own florist business.

Janice Chong

Founder of Floral Art and Design

LCGI, City & Guilds MDPF (Level 5 – British Master), Institute of Professional Florists (IoPF) Academic Fellow and RHS Chelsea Gold medallist, AIFD (US Master)

I trained with Royal Warrant Holders, Moyses Stevens in London, before spending five years creating flowers for Disney in Paris. I first came to the Academy as a student myself on the C&G Level 4 course. It opened my eyes to the world of floral design and the floristry industry as a whole. I just love teaching, sharing some of the skills, techniques and encouraging the students to achieve more than they thought possible.
