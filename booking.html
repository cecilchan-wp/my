We are operating in the form of moving stalls. Our business has a history of 16 years and is full of antiquity. It has been passed down to the second and third generations. All the skewers are selected by stall owner and marinated in a unique secret recipe, and then grilled with a special dip and the right fire. Now we will provide you with on-site barbecue service with traditional flavor and modern moving business service.
